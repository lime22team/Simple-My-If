About MyIF Timer – Fasting

MyIF Timer – Fasting is a simple, clean, and easy-to-use intermittent fasting companion designed to help you track your fasting and eating windows. Our goal is to offer a distraction-free experience that supports your daily routine without unnecessary complexity.

The app does not provide medical advice, nutritional guidance, or health recommendations. It only offers time-tracking features to help users follow their own fasting schedules. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making any changes to your diet or fasting habits.

MyIF Timer – Fasting does not collect personal data beyond what is required for the app to function locally on your device. All information stays on your device and is never shared with third parties.
