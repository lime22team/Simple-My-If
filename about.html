About – Simple My IF

Simple My IF is a clean, minimal, one-screen intermittent fasting timer designed for people who want a distraction-free fasting experience. No clutter, no unnecessary menus — just tap Start, track your fasting window easily, and stay consistent.

Core Values

  • Simplicity
  • Reliability
  • Zero friction
  • Privacy-friendly
  • No ads in the free version

This app does not collect personal data, does not track you, and does not require an account. Simple My IF is built for people who want a peaceful, focused fasting tool.
