About – MyIF Timer – Fasting

MyIF Timer – Fasting is a clean, minimal intermittent fasting timer designed by lime22team for people who want a distraction-free fasting experience. No clutter, no complex dashboards, no unnecessary menus — just one main screen, a simple fasting/eating cycle, and a clear countdown that helps you stay consistent.

Core values

  • Simplicity
  • Reliability
  • Zero friction
  • Privacy-first
  • No ads in the free version

What the app does

MyIF Timer – Fasting lets you:

  • Start and stop fasting with a single tap
  • Use simple presets like 16:8, 18:6 and more
  • Track both fasting and eating windows
  • See your recent fasting history and progress
  • Optionally unlock premium features for themes, auto cycles, and advanced stats

The app does not require an account, does not upload your data to a server, and does not track you. All fasting data stays on your device.
