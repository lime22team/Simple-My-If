This Privacy Policy explains how MyIF Timer – Fasting (“the App”) handles user information.

1. No Personal Data Collection

MyIF Timer – Fasting does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All data such as fasting times, history, theme preferences, and settings remain locally on your device.

2. No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising platforms, cloud services, or tracking systems. We do not share any information with external services or partners.

3. Local Storage Only

The App may store basic settings (such as selected fasting window, theme preferences, and history logs) using secure local storage technologies on your device. This data remains fully under your control.

4. Notifications

The App may send local notifications (timers, reminders). These notifications are handled entirely on the device and are not transmitted externally.

5. No Medical Advice

The App is not intended to diagnose, treat, or provide medical recommendations. For any health decisions, consult a qualified medical professional.

6. Children’s Privacy

MyIF Timer – Fasting is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children, as we do not collect any data at all.
