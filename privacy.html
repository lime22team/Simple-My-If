Privacy Policy – MyIF Timer – Fasting

Last updated: {insert date}

MyIF Timer – Fasting, developed by lime22team, is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The app stores the following locally on your device only:

  • Fasting start and end times
  • Fasting and eating durations
  • Selected fasting preset and settings
  • Theme preference
  • Premium purchase status

This information never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

No Accounts, No Servers, No Tracking

  • No account or login is required
  • No analytics SDKs are used
  • No advertising SDKs or trackers are used
  • No data is shared with third parties

Third-Party Services

MyIF Timer – Fasting does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data. In-app purchases are handled by Apple and Google via their official payment systems.

Data Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you want to delete your data, simply uninstall MyIF Timer – Fasting from your device. This will remove all stored fasting history and settings.

Children’s Privacy

MyIF Timer – Fasting is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our official project page or repository.
